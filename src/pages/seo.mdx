## Frequently Asked Questions

### How long does SEO take?
SEO is a long-term investment. You can expect to see initial improvements in 3-6 months, with more significant results building over 6-12 months.

### Do you guarantee rankings?
No one can guarantee specific rankings - search engines use complex, ever-changing algorithms. I guarantee transparent work, best practices, and measurable improvements in organic visibility.

### Can you help with local SEO?
Yes! Local SEO requires specific strategies like Google Business Profile optimization, local citations, and location-based content.

### What tools do you use?
I use a combination of industry-standard tools (Google Search Console, Analytics, Ahrefs, Screaming Frog) and custom scripts for analysis and monitoring.
